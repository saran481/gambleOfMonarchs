The gamble between Monarchs
for the Blue Throne

On the Islands of Dead Sea, there are four realms headed by four powerful Monarchs, The Spade King, The Diamond King, The Heart King and The Club King. Each of them is notorious and power mongering aspiring to expand their empire. Each Monarch wields a unique power and strategy to defeat the opponent. They possess mighty armies and ammunitions in their arsenals that could destroy the enemies into ashes.

For several years, each of these Monarchs has been trying to conquer the empire of the other three, and thus establish their domination on the land and water in the region. Each conspired against each other leading to several conflicts and bloodshed. Dramatically, all the four royals have allegiance to The Clown who guided the royal dynasties from during the turbulent times. Vowing to the promise made to the Clown, the four Monarchs had refrained from indulging in a destructive war against others.

Nevertheless, the Clown decided to reveal the secret to the Four Monarchs that was passed to him by his ancestors to reveal it to the

descendants of royalists under the right circumstances. On the far Islands of Dead Sea, there’s an ancient Imperial Kingdom that remains in shackles. There’s a throne in the capital of that Imperial Kingdom which possess supernatural powers, that it confers on its ascendants. The man who ascends it becomes the most powerful Man - in the military, wealth- on this part of the world. Nobody could ever defeat him and he attains immortality. The more battles he won, the more powerful he grows and more worlds he conquers.

“Now The reason why I am revealing this to all of you is, the throne has been craving for a sovereign to mount it, and its cravings have become aggressive that it would destroy our entire world had its cravings are not satiated. The one who ascends it becomes the Imperial Monarch, rest would turn into vassals to the Imperial Monarch,” said the Clown to the four Kings. This had triggered a competition and rivalry among the Kings to ascend the throne.
